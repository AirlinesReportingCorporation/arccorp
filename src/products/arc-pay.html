Get Started

ARC Pay offers efficient and effective options to process credit card transactions for all travel-related products and services.

Overview

Trusted by travel professionals for more than 25 years.

ARC Pay started as a way to add service fees to transactions and grew to help agencies process charges for air travel, group charges - any travel related payment that an agency might need to process. At ARC, travel is our business. We understand the nuances of the industry, including what many payment processors see as risks.

All Major Credit Cards Accepted.

All ARC Pay solutions accept and are fully supported by all the major credit card brands, allowing you the flexibility to focus on other things. for benchmarking.
Pricing

Simple, Transparent Pricing.

ARC Pay has a fixed fee of 3.5%, with a $0.70 minimum for all cards including UATP. There are no additional service or per transaction fees and no downgrade costs.

Features

Spend less time entering data.

Portfolio

Three convenient options to best fit the way you work.

Easy, Convenient Payment Processing

Issuing ARC Pay credit card payments via any GDS automatically pulls in ticket reservation data. You never have to re-enter passenger information or credit card numbers.

You process ARC Pay transactions as if you’re booking an airline ticket - using the ARC airline code “890”. This function is native within each of the main GDS providers - Amadeus, Sabre, Travelport (Apollo and Worldspan).

ARC Pay Agreement

Payment Processing for VTCs

ARC Pay Hub is an easy-to-use, web-based credit card processing tool for all travel-related credit card transactions. It’s ideal for smaller agencies or Verified Travel Consultants (VTCs) that need a simple payment workflow where GDS access or API integration is not available.

ARC Pay Hub functions as a browser-based “virtual terminal,” accessed via My ARC, that allows agencies to process transactions and view reports in a single place. Users can process transactions up to $10,000 that are settled via the normal ARC process and available through IAR on a daily basis.

Seamless Payment Acceptance

The ARC API gateway enables all popular payment methods and gives your customers a smooth one-click payment experience. The ARC Pay API helps agencies looking to shore up PCI compliance when capturing credit card information within their booking tools or company websites.

We are actively working on two “hosted” payment solutions. The Checkout version provides a full payment workflow in a lightbox environment allowing for a simplified user experience. The Session version will provide more flexibility by allowing users to embed sensitive Card and Exp fields within the checkout flow.
