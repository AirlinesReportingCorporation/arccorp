Overview

Powered by Global Air Sales

ARC is the trusted provider of settlement services between airlines and travel agencies, accrediting travel agencies, including travel management companies, leisure and online travel agencies, to sell airline tickets.
ARCs data is derived from processing these travel agency sales transactions, including refunds and exchanges, on behalf of the airlinesand therefore, is more accurate than search or reservation data.
ARCs ticketing data reflects both historical as well as all advance travel purchases.

All-inclusive view of Global Ticketing Data.

In partnership with the International Air Transport Association (IATA), ARC manages the worlds most accurate and comprehensive air transaction data. This dataset represents 57% of all trips taken globally,more than any other source of air passenger travel data. This data is comprised of sales made through airlines for approximately 100 global airlines and airline sales made through travel agencies from around the world for more than 290 additional airlines.
